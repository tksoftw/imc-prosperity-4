Bottom line:
- Smile residuals exist, but crossing spread is the gatekeeper.
- If delta-neutral fade is positive while cross fade is negative, trade it by quoting, not by panic-taking.
- If long-option hedged PnL is only positive in low-IV buckets, that is gamma scalp entry filter.
- Far-wing @0 prints are real tape events, but normal rank only rewards them if our resting bid can be hit.
